PT Userscripts


Automatic scripts that can be installed to make life easier.

1. Install a userscript manager:

Get Tampermonkey

2. Install a userscript:

YT Ad Skipper

Skips youtube video ads and hides ad banners. Bypasses youtube adblock detection because the script only hides the ads from view instead of removing them.

UNSW Anti Timeout

Automatically logins when Moodle or MyUNSW gets timed out. Is fully automatic if your device remembers your school account.

UNSW Timetable Today

Makes the site show the timetable of the current week instead of the first.

User Friendly Teams

Gets rid of the "use teams app" prompt and enables enlarging of the section content by clicking the left sidebar.

Thesaurus Word Replacer

Copy a word twice to replace it with synonyms from thesaurus.com.

UNSW Moodle Filter Tasks

Adds a filter button to the Moodle side menu to only show tasks.

YT Short Redirecter

Makes all youtube shorts play as a normal video.

AI Soullink

Remotely send requests to AI from another tab.
